TPDM Vol.3 - Section 3.5.5.13 (Route Symbol Arrangement), Section 3.5.7.42-3.5.5.46 (Cycle Symbols), Section 6.4 (Traffic Sign Symbols),

Regulatory Sign Symbol Usage

Used to indicate restriction of the road ahead

Restrictions applied might be height, width, no turning lef/right, no entry
