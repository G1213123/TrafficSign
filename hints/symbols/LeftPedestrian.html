Left Pedestrian Symbol

TPDM Vol.3 - Section 3.5.5.22-3.5.5.31 (Pedestrian Symbols), Section 3.5.4.15 (Symbol Spacing)

Left Pedestrian Symbol Usage

Used to indicate pedestrian facilities or pedestrian-related destinations

Typically placed adjacent to text indicating pedestrian areas or walkways

Symbol spacing: 2 s/w between symbols, 1.5 s/w between message and symbol (Section 3.5.4.15)
