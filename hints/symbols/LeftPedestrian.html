Left Pedestrian, Disabled and Cycle Symbol

TPDM Vol.3 - Section 3.5.5.34-3.5.5.35 (Pedestrian and Disab;ed Symbols), Section 3.5.5.47-3.5.5.49 (Cycle Symbols), Section 6.4 (Cycling signs),

Left Pedestrian Symbol Usage

Used to indicate pedestrian facilities or pedestrian-related destinations

Typically placed adjacent to text indicating pedestrian areas or walkways

Symbol facing left or right should align with the sign direction
