Lane Separation Line

TPDM Vol.3 - Section 3.5.6 (Gantry Signs), Section 3.5.4.9 (Border Spacings)

Lane Separation Line Usage

Used to separate different lanes vertically within a lane drop sign for gantry applications

Helps delineate individual lane destinations on overhead signs

Applied in lane destination signs with center arrangement format

Design Reference

Border spacing: 2.5 s/w from upper/side borders, 1.5 s/w from lower border (Section 3.5.4.9)

Center arrangement appropriate for lane destination signs (Section 3.5.4.10)

Line thickness should maintain visibility while not overwhelming the sign content

All dimensions subject to rounding as per Section 3.5.4.16
