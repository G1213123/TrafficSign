TPDM Vol.3 4.2 - Text Font Standards

Transport Font Family

Choose the appropriate font weight based on text prominence and readability requirements:

TransportMedium - Standard text weight

TransportHeavy - Bold text weight

Font Usage Guidelines:
  • TransportMedium: Standard weight for most text elements, provides good readability
  • TransportHeavy: Bold weight for emphasis, warnings, or primary destinations
