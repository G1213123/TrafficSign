Text Placement Guidelines

Text placement follows TPDM specifications for legend block formation and message alignment:

Words are formed by butting tiles together with 2 s/w space between words (Section 3.5.4.1)

English messages are butted above corresponding Chinese messages (Section 3.5.4.3)

Alignment: Left for stack/flag/map type signs, center for gantry signs (Section 3.5.4.10)
