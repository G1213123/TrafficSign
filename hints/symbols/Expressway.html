Expressway Symbol

TPDM Vol.3 - Section 3.5.7.18-3.5.7.21 (Expressway Symbols), Section 3.5.4.6 (Green Panels)

Expressway symbols should only be used for signs located not in an expressway.

i.e. the sign is in blue background and contains a green panel directing to the expressway.

This sign is used to warn motorist the start of expressway, which additional regulations will be applied.
