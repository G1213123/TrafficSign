TPDM Vol.3 2.4 - Main Road Termination Types

Main Road Shape Options

Different termination shapes are used depending on the traffic flow and road design requirements:

  • Arrow: Standard continuation indicator
  • Stub: Abrupt termination point
  • RedBar: Prohibition or stop indication
  • LaneDrop: Gradual lane reduction with corner radius control
  • T-Junction: Three-way intersection
  • Y-Junction: Branching road at angle
