Gantry Destination Line

TPDM Vol.3 - Section 3.5.6 (Gantry Signs), Diagram 3.5.6.14 (Combined Direction Signs)

Gantry Destination Line Usage

When two or more lanes serve not just single destination but multiple shared destinations

Horizontal line to inform that all connected lanes are well served by each destination

Used in combined direction signs where destinations apply to multiple lanes

Design Reference

Center arrangement appropriate for gantry signs in mainline format (Section 3.5.4.10)

Separation of destinations: comma for horizontal layout, no separator for vertical layout (Section 3.5.4.13)

Arrow positioning: above the center of the traffic lane to which it refers (Section 3.5.6.9)

Spacing between symbols: as specified in Diagram 3.5.4.3
