Gantry Border Divider

TPDM Vol.3 - Section 3.5.6 (Gantry Signs), Section 3.5.4.8 (Sign Border Details)

Gantry Border Divider Usage

Used to separate content horizontally within gantry-type borders for overhead directional signs

Each of the divided compartment of gantry shall represent approximate the traffic lane width

Follows center arrangement principles for gantry signs in mainline or "lane drop" format (Section 3.5.4.10)

Select the divider when placing Gantry border to assign the divider height

Design Reference

Border width: 1.5 s/w for directional signs as per Table 3.5.4.1

Corner radii: Internal 1.5 s/w, External 3 s/w for directional signs

Spacing: 2.5 s/w from upper/side borders, 1.5 s/w from lower border (Section 3.5.4.9)

Sign width extension should not exceed 25% of lane width (Section 3.5.6.8)
