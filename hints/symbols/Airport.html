Airport Symbol

TPDM Vol.3 - Section 3.5.7.22-3.5.7.25 (Airport Symbols), Section 3.5.4.15 (Symbol Spacing)

Airport Symbol Usage

Refer to general symbols (see Tunnel Symbol) for placement and spaing

Orientation should rotate to align with destination direction for clarity

However, airplane direction should never point downwards for safety recognition

Spacing: 2 s/w between symbols, 1.5 s/w between message and symbol (Section 3.5.4.15)
