Route Shield Symbol

TPDM Vol.3 - Section 3.5.6 (Gantry Signs), Section 3.5.4.15 (Symbol Spacing), Section 3.5.4.9 (Border Spacing)

Route Shield Symbol Usage

Used to identify specific route numbers and highway designations

Can be positioned adjacent to text, below text, or with directional arrows

Spacing: 1.5 s/w bottom spacing extension applies to route shields (Section 3.5.4.9)

Symbol spacing: 2 s/w between symbols, proper alignment with destination text
