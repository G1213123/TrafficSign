Transport Font Family Selection (TPDM Vol.3 Section 3.5.3)

Font selection follows TPDM specifications for optimal legibility and consistency:

Dark colour panels use Transport Medium alphabet with white color (Section 3.5.4.6)

Light colour use Transport Heavy alphabet with black color (Section 3.5.4.7)
