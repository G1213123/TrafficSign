Border Colour Purpose

TPDM Vol.3 – Section 3.2.7 (Colour formats), 3.5.4.8 (Sign Border Details)

When to use each scheme
Scheme Background Message Typical purpose
Green Background Green White Signs within expressway; panels directing to expressways.
Blue Background Blue White Non-expressway and regional destination route guidance.
White Background White Black Local destination blocks and general information panels.
White Background - Parking White Black Parking direction only border.
Yellow Background Yellow Black Temporary traffic management or special event directions.
Brown Background Brown White Tourist attractions and recreational destinations.
Red Background Red White Special cases for warning message. Should not be used in normal situations.
Notes
  • Border colour matches the message colour unless specified (e.g., Dark border background use white message colour).
  • Message colour follows background luminance: dark backgrounds → white message; light backgrounds → black message.
  • For text alphabet choice, see Message Colour hint (Transport Medium/Heavy usage).
