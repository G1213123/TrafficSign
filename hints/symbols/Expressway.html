Expressway Symbol

TPDM Vol.3 - Section 3.5.7.22-3.5.7.25 (Expressway Symbols), Section 3.5.4.6 (Green Panels)

Expressway symbols should only be used for signs located not in an expressway.

i.e. the sign is in blue background and contains a green panel directing to the expressway.

Green panel specifications: white border, green background, white messages in Transport Medium alphabet (Section 3.5.4.6)
